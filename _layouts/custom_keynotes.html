Keynote 1 Session Chair: Themis Palpanas (Université Paris Cité)

Alphabets, Grammars, Calculators, and the End of Hand-Crafted Systems

Stratos Idreos (Harvard University)
▾
Abstract ▾
Data-centric computation is evolving rapidly as new data types, hardware and workloads appear. This talk sketches a path from hand-crafted systems to automated “system calculators” that compose storage formats, operators and architectures on demand to match each workload.
Bio ▾
Stratos Idreos is Professor of Computer Science at Harvard University. He directs the Harvard Data Systems Laboratory and works on data-centric system design that adapts to hardware and workloads. His work has been recognized with awards including early-career honors and contributions awards in the data systems community.
